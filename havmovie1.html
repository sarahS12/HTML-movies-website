Kiki's Delivery Service

Kiki’s Delivery Service is another popular Studio Ghibli Film about a 13-year-old witch who moves to a seaside town with her cat, Jiji. Completely alone in a new place (as per a witch tradition) for a year, she tries to master flying on a broom by setting up a delivery service with the lady who runs the bakery she’s staying in. As she delivers packages she makes new friends, including an artist who lives alone in the woods names Ursula and and old lady named Madame who makes Kiki a cake for her birthday. During her journey, her insecurity gets the best of her and she loses her ability to properly do magic, and has to over come her insecurities to regain her powers back.
