Castle in the Sky

Title Castle in the Sky
Director Hayao Miyazaki
Screenplay Hayao Miyazaki
Music Joe Hisaishi

Description

Castle in the Sky is a Japanese animated film directed by Hayao Miyazaki. It tells the story of a young girl named Sheeta who possesses a mysterious crystal pendant. She meets a young boy named Pazu, and together they embark on an adventure to find the floating castle Laputa while evading government agents and sky pirates. The film explores themes of friendship, courage, and the preservation of nature.

Cast

  • Sheeta - Mayumi Tanaka
  • Pazu - James Van Der Beek
  • Muska - Mark Hamill
  • Dola - Cloris Leachman
