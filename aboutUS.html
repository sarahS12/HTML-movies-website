about family flix

FamilyFlix is a website to Watch and view detail information about movies falling under 3 categories: family, adventure and factional, where the information includes a synopsis of the movie, a movie trailer, a link to watch the movie, and a movie evaluation form. Family Flex aims to be a leading platform for animation movie lovers and watch them in a family environment in a safe and comfortable manner.
