The Cat Returns

Haru Yoshioka is your normal high school girl that likes to be a troublemaker but is kind at heart. One day one the way home her kindness made her save a cat from being run over and this one act changed her life dramatically. It turned out the cat she saved was actually the prince of cats, and for saving his life, his dad offered his hand in marriage to which she “said yes” to and hence got turned into a catgirl. Will Haru get her life back or stay as a cat forever, find out through the amazing adventure she has to take to get her old life back

Length: 1hr. 15min
Aired: 2002
Genres: Adventure-Drama-Fantasy
Rating: G-All Ages
